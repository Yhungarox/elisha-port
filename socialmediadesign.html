Social Media Design

Project Type

Date

Role

Wayforward Global Logistics needed a dynamic social media presence to showcase their global shipping services, including freight, sea, air cargo, and local logistics, while promoting their new office in Lagos, Nigeria. I developed 16 creative social media designs highlighting their offerings, mobile app, 24/7 Availability, and Door to door delivery.

The designs maintained brand consistency with clean layouts, bold visuals, and engaging content tailored to resonate with their diverse audience. These assets boosted their online engagement and reinforced their position as a reliable, innovative logistics brand.
