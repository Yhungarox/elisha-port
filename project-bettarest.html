BettaRest Brand Identity

Logo and Visual Identity Design

Project Overview

The BettaRest brand identity design project focused on creating a logo and visual identity that embody hospitality, relaxation, reliability, and connection. Drawing inspiration from a palm tree for assurance, interconnected elements for networking, and wavy lines for calmness, the design conveys BettaRest's mission to offer a seamless and restful hotel booking experience. The project aimed to establish a professional, approachable, and memorable brand image for the platform.
